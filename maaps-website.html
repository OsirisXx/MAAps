About MAAPS

Empowering Filipino Farmers with Data Intelligence

MAAPS (Market Analytics of Agricultural Products Service) is a Nova Tech Inc. product designed specifically for small-scale Filipino farmers. We transform complex market data into actionable insights, helping farmers make informed decisions about what to plant, when to sell, and how to maximize their profits.

Our mission is to bridge the information gap that has long disadvantaged small agricultural holders, giving them the same market intelligence that large agribusinesses have always enjoyed.

Planning

Seasonal Calendar

Know the best times to plant and harvest different crops throughout the year.

🌸 Dry Season (Mar-May)

Best Crops:

  • Corn, Mungbean, Peanut
  • Watermelon, Cantaloupe
  • Onion, Garlic

Tips: Focus on drought-resistant varieties. Ensure adequate irrigation.

🌧️ Wet Season (Jun-Nov)

Best Crops:

  • Rice (main crop)
  • Vegetables, Leafy greens
  • Root crops, Ginger

Tips: Prepare drainage systems. Watch for pest outbreaks.

🍂 Cool Dry (Dec-Feb)

Best Crops:

  • Strawberries, Lettuce
  • Cabbage, Broccoli
  • Carrots, Potatoes

Tips: Ideal for highland vegetables. Great for seedling preparation.

Common Questions

Questions About MAAPS?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our support team anytime.

📊
Who is MAAPS designed for? +
MAAPS is built specifically for small-scale agricultural farm holders aged 18 and above, located in rural provinces. We currently focus on sweet potato farmers but plan to expand to other crops as we grow.
What is the monthly subscription cost? +
Our base subscription starts at ₱500 per month. We offer tiered plans to match different farm sizes and feature needs. Training sessions are available at ₱300 per session for additional skill-building.
How does the Agri Prediction feature work? +
Our prediction engine analyzes historical price data, current market trends, supply volumes, and seasonal patterns to forecast future product conditions. This helps you plan planting and selling timelines with data-backed confidence.
What is the Farmer to Farmer connection feature? +
This feature connects you with other MAAPS farmers in your region. By coordinating harvests, sharing market intelligence, and negotiating collectively, small farms can operate with the leverage of a large agricultural enterprise.
Is my farm data kept private? +
Yes. Individual farm data is always kept confidential. Any data monetization uses fully anonymized, aggregated datasets. Your specific farm details are never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
